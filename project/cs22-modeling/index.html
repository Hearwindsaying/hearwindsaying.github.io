3D Digital Modeling FA22

Jul 15, 2020

Here are some of selected shots from Prof. Lorie Loeb’s modeling class1 I took in Fall 22. Interestingly, I also took Prof. Wojciech Jarosz’s rendering algorithms in FA22 and they are somewhat complementary to each other. Rendering Algorithms tells us how to render photorealistic images given the scene and Digital Modeling tells us how to author the scene for a renderer and use the renderer from an artistic perspective.

Classroom

Abstract

My abstract design is inspired by a talk at SIGGRAPH 2015 – Big Hero 6: Into the Portal. They author this portal using a variation of Mandelbrot fractals with volumetric path tracing. I went in another direction instead by applying deformers to geometry.
